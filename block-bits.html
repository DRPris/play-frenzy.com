About Block Bits

Block Bits is a captivating blocky adventure game that combines the best elements of survival, mining, and creativity. With its charming low-poly aesthetic and intuitive gameplay, Block Bits offers endless possibilities for players of all ages.

Explore vast procedurally generated worlds, gather resources, craft tools and structures, and survive challenging environments. Whether you want to build magnificent structures or embark on exciting adventures, the choice is yours!

Every world in Block Bits is unique, with different biomes, landscapes, and resources to discover. From towering mountains to deep caves filled with valuable minerals, there's always something new to explore and uncover.

Game Details

  • Genre: Survival, Crafting, Adventure
  • Developer: Kay Lousberg
  • Features: Block building, resource gathering, crafting
  • Platform: Browser (PC & Mobile)
  • Players: Single-player
  • Release Date: 2023

How to Play

  1. Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  2. Click to break blocks and gather resources
  3. Open your inventory (E key) to craft items
  4. Place blocks by right-clicking
  5. Press ESC to access the game menu

Starting Tips

  • Gather wood first to craft basic tools
  • Build a shelter before nightfall
  • Mine stone to upgrade your tools
  • Collect food to maintain your energy
  • Explore caves carefully and bring torches
