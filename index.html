Strategies & Tips

Pro Tips for Beginners

  1. Focus on Card Synergy - Cards that complement each other are more effective than just using high-power cards.
  2. Complete Daily Missions - These provide valuable resources and rare cards to strengthen your deck.
  3. Understand Card Types - Different cards have unique strengths against specific opponents. Learn the counter system.
  4. Conserve Resources - Don't spend all your energy in one turn. Sometimes it's better to save for a powerful combo.
  5. Upgrade Strategically - Focus on upgrading cards you use frequently rather than spreading upgrades across many cards.

Special Combo: Fire & Ice

Combine the "Frost Mage" card with "Fire Elemental" to create a devastating freeze-burn effect that deals damage over time while slowing enemy attacks.
