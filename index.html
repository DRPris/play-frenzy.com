About Block Bits

Block Bits is a captivating blocky adventure game that combines the best elements of survival, mining, and creativity. With its charming low-poly aesthetic and intuitive gameplay, Block Bits offers endless possibilities for players of all ages.

Explore vast procedurally generated worlds, gather resources, craft tools and structures, and survive challenging environments. Whether you want to build magnificent structures or embark on exciting adventures, the choice is yours!

Game Details

  • Genre: Survival, Crafting, Adventure
  • Developer: Kay Lousberg
  • Features: Block building, resource gathering, crafting
  • Platform: Browser (PC & Mobile)
  • Players: Single-player

How to Play

  1. Click on the game above to start playing directly in your browser
  2. Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  3. Click to break blocks and gather resources
  4. Open your inventory to craft items and place blocks
  5. Build structures to protect yourself and store your items
  6. Explore the world to find rare resources and discover new biomes
